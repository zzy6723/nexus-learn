# Synthetic Relation Lecture 001: Gradient Descent and Gradient Ascent

**Created:** 2026-07-12

---

Gradient descent and gradient ascent are iterative optimisation methods with opposite objectives. Gradient descent moves against the gradient to reduce an objective, whereas gradient ascent moves with the gradient to increase it. The two methods therefore contrast in both update direction and optimisation goal.
