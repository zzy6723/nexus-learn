# Gradient In Multivariable Calculus

The gradient of a differentiable scalar function is the vector of its partial derivatives. In coordinates, the gradient formula is

\[
\nabla f(x) = \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_1},\ldots,\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_d}\right).
\]

The gradient is a mathematical concept, while the coordinate expression is a Formula that formalizes it.
